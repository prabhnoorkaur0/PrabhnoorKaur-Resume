Hello everyone! My name is Prabhnoor Kaur, and I am currently a fourth year student at the University of California, Riverside. I am pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science with a concentration in Law and Society. A little background about me, I was born and raised in India for more than a decade. In 2016, I immigrated to the United States and have been here ever since then. I am fluent in multiple languages, which helps me day to day to interact with peers on campus.

In early 2023, I attended an internship in Washington, DC. I was an intern for House Representative Katie Porter. My experience interning at her office was life changing and the people I met there were amazing. Interning at Katie Porter’s office gave me some important insight on the inner workings of what exactly a house representative does. It gave me more information as to how the world of politics works in the city. The experience also allowed me to explore the city where the heart of our government is based and operates. There were also a large number of museums all around the city that allowed me to learn about many things and explore the culture found in the city.

I also have extensive experience working at the Dining Services at the University of California, Riverside. I have been an employee there for two years already. At first, I started off as a regular worker, then was promoted to student lead after my first year of working there. This has shaped the leadership skills i use to this day.
